About Native Plants of Ontario

Dedicated to preserving and promoting Ontario's natural heritage through education and awareness.

Our Mission

Native Plants of Ontario is dedicated to promoting the preservation, cultivation, and appreciation of indigenous plant species throughout Ontario. We aim to provide comprehensive resources that educate the public about the ecological importance of native plants and encourage their use in gardens and landscapes.

Our Vision

We envision a future where Ontario's native plants are widely recognized, valued, and integrated into both natural and developed landscapes. Through education and advocacy, we strive to create a community that understands the vital role native plants play in supporting local ecosystems and biodiversity.

Meet Our Founder

Malcolm Grace

Founder & Lead Horticulturist

Malcolm is a horticulturist with over 5 years of experience in landscaping industry. After completing his diploma in Horticultural Industries at Algonquin College, Malcolm worked with various companies across Ottawa.

His passion for native plants began during childhood explorations in the forests of Northern Ontario. This early fascination evolved into a career dedicated to preserving Ontario's natural botanical heritage.

In 2024, Malcolm founded Native Plants of Ontario to share his knowledge and create a comprehensive resource for enthusiasts, gardeners, and students alike.

Our Values

Conservation

We believe in the importance of preserving Ontario's native plant species and their habitats for future generations.

Education

We are committed to providing accurate, accessible information about native plants to educate and inspire our community.

Community

We foster a supportive community of gardeners, naturalists, and environmentalists who share knowledge and experiences.
