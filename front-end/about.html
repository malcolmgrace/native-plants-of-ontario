About

Native Plants of Ontario is a resource dedicated to showcasing the diverse flora native to Ontario. Explore plant profiles, learn about their habitats and uses, and discover their ecological importance. Perfect for enthusiasts, gardeners, and students, this site highlights the beauty and significance of Ontario's native plants.


Website Creator

This website was created by Malcolm Grace, a passionate horticulturist and web developer. Malcolm has a deep love for the native plants of Ontario and aims to share his knowledge and enthusiasm through this resource.
